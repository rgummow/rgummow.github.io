Audience

Target Audience

My target audience will be people living in Rexburg, ID. Specifically, students, hikers, athletes, explorers, and shoppers.

Personas

Persona 1: A student between the age of 19-25 who likes to explore and go on adventures no matter what the weather and uses an iPhone.

Persona 2: An adult at the age of 50 who has a fairly good income and uses a desktop computer/laptop. They like to go out, work outside, and go shopping and have to rely on the weather forecast.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: A man decides to take his family on a camping trip and is trying to decide what day the weather will be the best.

Scenario 2: A student is traveling home and wants to know what the weather is like to get to their destination.

Scenario 3: A soccer student has a game on Saturday and wants to know if the weather will be okay to play.

Scenario 4: A mom wants to go to the store to pickup her child's birthday present. She wants to know if she can get there okay if it snows.

Scenario 5: A woman wants to go on a hike this weekend and is trying to decide if she can make it work.
