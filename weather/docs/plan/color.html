Color

Background Color

The background color will be a off-white color. Specifically, #EBDDFE. The header's background color, however will be white.

Text Color

The text color will be a dark navy such as #1B00B1.

Links

The colors of the links will be a red-orange color, specifically, #EBDDFE. When they are hovered over, they will change to a light cream color, #FFF9E8.
