Design Principles

PARC: Proximity - Pinterest

The Pinterest website demonstrates proximity by aligning the content with other items that are similar. Then each section of content is staggered from the others to show they are separate links, but still related.

PARC: Alignment - Church of Jesus Christ

On the Church of Jesus Christ website, all of the titles are left-aligned. This helps to show they are similar items that do not necessarily belong together.

PARC: Repetition - Jane

Jane.com demonstrates repetition in the structure of their page. As you scroll down the page, all of the products are displayed as a square photograph. This helps to tie individual elements together and create some consistency with the page.

PARC: Contrast - The Wall Street Journal

The Wall Street Journal exemplifies contrast by having black text on a white background. This helps the viewer to easily read the content on the page. It also helps to emphasize elements on the page such as titles and other important content.
